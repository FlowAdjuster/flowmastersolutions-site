About FlowMaster LLC

Innovation, efficiency, and compliance — built for the insurance adjusting industry.

Who We Are

FlowMaster LLC is a technology company dedicated to building streamlined, secure, and highly efficient tools for insurance adjusters. Our solutions reduce administrative overhead, eliminate wasted travel time, and increase productivity through automation and intelligent routing.

What We Do

We engineer software that empowers adjusters to work smarter, not harder. From real-time route optimization to automated claim squeezing, FlowMaster tools are designed to assist adjusters in managing high-volume claim loads without sacrificing accuracy or policyholder satisfaction.

Our flagship product, FlowAdjuster, is built on modern architecture with a heavy focus on performance, data integrity, and operational reliability.

Our Commitment

FlowMaster Solutions is committed to full compliance, secure handling of customer data, and transparency in how our systems operate. We follow best practices for security, audit logging, and identity management.
