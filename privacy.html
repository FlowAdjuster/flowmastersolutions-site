Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters. Here’s how FlowMaster handles data responsibly.

Information We Collect

FlowMaster LLC collects only the information necessary to provide routing, scheduling, and messaging services through the FlowAdjuster platform. This may include adjuster account information, claim data, timestamps, and usage logs.

How We Use Information

All data is used exclusively to support claim routing, scheduling, communication, and application functionality. We do not sell, share, or disclose data to third parties for marketing or advertising.

Data Security

FlowMaster implements industry-standard security measures, including encryption in transit, secure authentication, role-based access, and audit logging. Only authorized users can access controlled information.

Policyholder Data

Policyholder data used within FlowAdjuster is limited to information needed for inspection scheduling, routing, and communication. This is handled in accordance with applicable privacy expectations and carrier requirements.

Your Choices

Users may contact FlowMaster at any time to request account changes, data clarification, or other inquiries related to privacy.
